Site Name

DnD MeetUp

  • This name is for a general MeetUp site for DnD players. It offers locations, character designs, and a how to play section.

Optional Domain Availability: dndmeetup.net

Purpose

For new and old DnD players

  • The site provides a "How-to-play" guide, a character creation collection, and a few locations to meetup and play DnD.

Scenarios

  • How do I find and contact my MeetUp group?
  • I know nothing about DnD. Where do I start?
